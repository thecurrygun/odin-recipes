Description Jack's Wrap

Best Wrap ever mate!

Ingredients

  • 4 each Mission® 96% Fat Free Burrito Size Flour Tortillas
  • 4 breast half, bone and skin removed (blank)s boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • ½ cup fat-free Italian dressing
  • ½ cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • ½ pound shredded vegan Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup peeled and seeded cucumber, chopped

Steps

    Step 1: Marinate chicken in dressing 2 hours to overnight.
    Step 2: Combine tomatoes, olives, cucumber, lemon juice, and 1/2 cup of the sour cream mixture. Reserve.
    Step 3: Grill chicken, let cool, and cut into very thin strips.
    Step 4: Spread each tortilla with 1 tablespoon sour cream mixture.
